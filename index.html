To get started, please make sure you have downloaded the template using the "Download Template" button. Upload the file to your OneDrive account and open it in Excel online. Select a project to load that project's information into your worksheet (releases, users, etc). Indents are done with the ">" character. After your requirement fields are filled out, click "send to spira" to create the requirements in SpiraTeam. New requirements must have their "Req #" field empty or they will be ignored.
